---

**Option 3: For Colleagues/Team Members (Slightly more personal, but still professional)**

**Subject: Happy Diwali to You and Your Family!**

Dear Team/Colleagues,

I wanted to take a moment to send my warmest wishes to all of you as we approach Diwali, the festival of lights.

May this festive season bring immense joy, peace, and prosperity to you and your families. I hope you get to enjoy this special time with your loved ones, celebrating with light, laughter, and happiness.

**Key things to remember when sending:**

*   **Personalize:** Always use the recipient's name where possible.
*   **Timing:** Send it a day or two before the main Diwali celebration for maximum impact.
*   **Tone:** Keep it positive, respectful, and appropriate for your professional relationship.
*   **Proofread:** Double-check for any typos or grammatical errors.
